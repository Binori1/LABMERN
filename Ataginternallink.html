TATA Motoros


Tata Motors is India's leading automaker, part of the Tata Group conglomerate, manufacturing everything from commercial trucks/buses to popular passenger cars like the Nexon and Safari, with a strong focus on high-tech, sustainable e-mobility solutions (Ziptron) and a global presence via Jaguar Land Rover, driving innovation in the Indian automotive sector with human-centric, safe, and stylish designs.  
Key Aspects:
Market Position: Largest commercial vehicle (CV) maker in India and a top three player in passenger vehicles (PV), leading the EV segment. 
Product Range: Offers a diverse portfolio including SUVs (Harrier, Safari), hatchbacks (Altroz, Tiago), EVs (Nexon EV, Punch EV), trucks, buses, and luxury vehicles through JLR. 
Technology & Innovation: Known for pioneering indigenous cars (Indica), cutting-edge features, safety (IMPACT Design), smart mobility, and advanced Ziptron EV tech. 
Global Presence: Owns Jaguar Land Rover (JLR) and has a global footprint in over 175 countries. 
Sustainability Focus: Committed to a greener future with a strong push into electric vehicles and alternative fuels like hydrogen. 
Structure: Recently demerged its CV and PV businesses for focused growth, operating through entities like Tata Passenger Electric Mobility (TPEM).
